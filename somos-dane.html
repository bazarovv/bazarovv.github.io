Introduction

We designed a digital platform where all the employees of the Colombian Department of Statistics can share their knowledge and the best work practices.

The main objective of the project was to build knowledge-based digital community, where they users could learn and instruct abilities related with their daily activities.

First approach

User analysis

Make and analysis of the user needs and goals was the first step of our project approaching. We detected opportunities to improve the structure of the platform in order to provide accurate experiences to the users.

Interaction flow

Given aspects such as the content of the portal, the business goals and several usability reviews were designed the structure of each page and the user interaction flow.
